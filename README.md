## What We Are

Meraki Studios is a creative tech studio building games, tools, and eventually the engine underneath both — one phase at a time.

We're not chasing trends. No AI-generated frames, no borrowed engines, no bloated software that does everything poorly. Just a team that cares too much about how things feel to take the easy route.

We started in Minecraft. We're not stopping there.

---

## The Roadmap

We're building this studio the same way we build our projects — from the ground up.

### 🟢 Phase 1 — The Minecraft Saga *(current)*
This is where we prove we can ship. Polished Minecraft projects, tools the community actually needs, and a card game that has no right to be this fun.

- **Liar's Table** — A one-of-a-kind multiplayer card game built entirely inside Minecraft
- **WorldChatter 4** — A complete chat overhaul rebuilt from scratch
- **GeminiCraft** — Google's Gemini AI, living inside your Minecraft server *(full release)*

### ⚪ Phase 2A — The Language Milestone
We stop borrowing tools. We build our own.

- **Vyn** — Our own scripting language
- **Vynium** — Vyn with full Spigot & Paper API support
- A Minecraft plugin game made entirely with Vynium

### ⚪ Phase 2B — The Store Milestone
The studio gets its own marketplace — runs independently of Vyn, launches when Phase 1 products are ready to sell.

- **Meraki Studios: Store** — Official marketplace for our projects

### ⚪ Phase 3 — The Vyn Saga
We build the engine our games run on.

- **VynEngine** — Our in-house game engine, powered by Vyn *(scoped to our games, not a general-purpose engine)*
- A full video game built entirely with VynEngine
- Software tools, built on our own foundation

### ⚪ Phase 4 — The Studio Saga
Meraki becomes a full software company — multiple products, engines, games, and tools, all built on what we made ourselves.

## How Our Projects Work

Not everything we make is a Meraki Studios product — and that's intentional.

### Studio-Made Projects
Projects planned, built, and owned by Meraki Studios. These can be free or paid. Everyone who worked on it earns a share of monthly revenue (after a 10% studio cut). The studio has final say.

### Passion-Made Projects
Things the team makes for fun — still get the Meraki name, subdomain, website listing, and social reach. They're always free (ad revenue is fair game). Owned by whoever made them.
